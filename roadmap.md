# Monedario Growth Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Acquisition Engine

**Goal**  
Strengthen Monedario's SEO foundations so existing content and calculators can capture search demand more efficiently.

**Strategic rationale**  
Monedario already has meaningful content breadth, calculator utility, and trust signals. The immediate bottleneck is not lack of assets, but weak packaging of those assets into a clear search-intent architecture. This phase improves discoverability, metadata quality, homepage positioning, and measurement.

**Expected outcomes**
- Clearer homepage value proposition for Chile-focused financial education.
- Better alignment between target queries, landing pages, and metadata.
- Lower index dilution from low-value taxonomy surfaces.
- Search Console and analytics baselines ready for weekly monitoring.

**Key metrics to monitor**
- Total impressions and clicks in Google Search Console.
- Non-brand clicks for priority queries in Chile.
- Homepage CTR from search.
- Indexed pages versus submitted pages.
- Organic entrances to calculators and top articles.

## Phase 2 - Authority Building

**Goal**  
Turn Monedario's existing article and calculator inventory into defensible topic clusters around the financial decisions Chilean users actually search for.

**Strategic rationale**  
Once the acquisition layer is in place, growth will depend on building clear topical authority. Monedario should win a focused set of recurring decisions first: sueldo, AFP/APV, crédito, cesantía, UF, impuestos, and presupuesto. The objective is to create cluster hubs, tighten internal linking, and refresh high-intent assets.

**Expected outcomes**
- Stronger topical authority around core Chilean personal finance decisions.
- Higher internal-link density between guides, calculators, glossary, and legal explainers.
- Better user navigation from discovery page to decision-support page.
- Improved freshness and completeness on high-intent evergreen content.

**Key metrics to monitor**
- Query breadth and page-level impressions by cluster.
- Average position for cluster hub pages.
- Internal clicks from articles to calculators.
- Organic sessions landing on cluster hubs.
- Scroll depth and engagement on refreshed high-intent pages.

## Phase 3 - Conversion and Distribution Readiness

**Goal**  
Add an owned-audience and conversion layer so search traffic compounds into repeat visits, email capture, and disciplined distribution.

**Strategic rationale**  
Distribution should start only after Monedario's landing pages and topic architecture are strong enough to convert attention into deeper engagement. This phase adds newsletter or email capture, intent-based CTAs, and a lightweight reporting cadence that supports controlled promotion.

**Expected outcomes**
- Repeatable email or newsletter entry points across the site.
- Consistent CTA structure by page intent.
- Weekly decision-making based on measurable SEO and engagement data.
- Distribution experiments that amplify already-strong assets instead of weak ones.

**Key metrics to monitor**
- Email signup conversion rate by template and channel.
- CTA click-through rate from homepage, articles, and calculators.
- Returning visitor share.
- Traffic contribution from controlled social distribution.
- Weekly movement of the keyword watchlist.
